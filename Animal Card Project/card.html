The Tiger

The tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the Genus Panthera native to Asia.

  • Eats: Wild boar, Sambar deer
  • Mass: 200-680lbs.(Male, Adult) 140-370lbs. (Female Adult)
  • Speed: 30-40mph
  • Lifespan: 10-15 years

There are nine types of tigers in the world. Six endangered and 3 extincted. The Bengal Tiger, the White Tiger, the Siberian Tiger, the Sumatran Tiger, the Indochinese Tiger, the Malayan Tiger, the South China Tiger all still live and the Casplan Tiger, the Bali Tiger and the Javan Tiger are extincted. Between 7,000 and 10,000 tigers can be found right here in the United States. There;s over 100,000 that actually live in the wild.
